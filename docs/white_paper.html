White Paper 2026

GHOSTSignal: Values Based Advertising Is World Making

VISUAL: Harmony not Hype Concept: Abstract static/noise transitioning into a clear, sharp signal beam in Persian Green.

Summary

The current digital advertising model is built on a faceless, transactional foundation that prioritizes reach over resonance, creating distrust and wasted attention.

This paper presents the rationale for a new model: Values-Based Advertising. By first understanding the world-making power of our values, then establishing a standard of membership, and finally curating partnerships through a deep, bespoke compatibility framework, we can create an ecosystem that is not only more profitable, but also more meaningful. This is the business case for soulful advertising.

Part 1: The Delicate Inevitability of Making the World

The Neglect and Opportunity of Worldview
VISUAL: The Weaver / The Architect Concept: Close-up of hands weaving a tapestry or playing a cello. Warm Terra Cotta tones.

Our lives are lived according to the narratives we inhabit. These stories shape our values, which in turn guide our decisions. As the philosopher Alasdair MacIntyre argued, our choices are intelligible only within a shared narrative. This principle is not merely academic; it is the fundamental engine of human action. Every day, with every choice, our decisions make the world. This gives every creator and every brand the profound possibility to make the world one way or another.

Today’s culture is marked by competing narratives and fragmented attention. Into this landscape enters advertising, a powerful force that can either add to the distortion or help harmonize like-storied partners. The dominant model, however, has abdicated its world-making responsibility. We call this “The Static” (in distinction from “The Signal”) — a cacophony of disruptive and misaligned messaging.

“He who has a Why to live for can bear almost any How.” — Nietzsche

Values-based advertising is a system that can honor this reality: resisting the gravity to exploit scarcity, it can promote flourishing. Saint Augustine 2000 years ago spoke of the heart’s restlessness; we want to push against messaging that leverages restlessness, and want to speak to that better part of ourselves that aspires, dreams, and retains the big picture, ‘the why’.

Part 2: The GHOSTSignal Standard

A Membership Doorway to Fidelity
VISUAL: The Temple / The Doorway Concept: A surreal, beautiful doorway standing in a mist. Regal Royal Wine lighting.

The first step is to recognize that our values architect the world. The second is to build a practical structure upon that foundation. If the dominant advertising model is a wide-open, faceless marketplace, GHOSTSignal is a curated ecosystem entered through a specific doorway: Membership.

We call our new model Values-Based Advertising, or simply, The Signal. The signal is always present, but as a spirit, a specter, a ghost signal: one must have ears to hear it through the static.

The Doorway to GHOSTSignal Membership:

  • Values - “Same Page” World Making: Creators and contributors should avoid promoting ideas or products that diminish human dignity.
  • Authenticity - Character & Relational Trust: Creators and contributors seek to embody the values they express: character, transparency, humility.
  • Flourishing - Impact & Hope: Creators and advertisers aim to produce work that leaves people “net better off”.
“Put first things first and we get second things thrown in...” — C.S. Lewis

Part 5: Building A World-Making Ecosystem

VISUAL: The Orchestra / The Constellation Concept: A constellation of stars connecting against a deep Royal Wine sky.

The ultimate vision of GHOSTSignal extends beyond individual partnerships. The true, world-making potential is realized when these connections interact, creating a network effect that transforms our members into a genuine community.

  • Creator-to-Creator Synergy: Feed swaps and guest appearances.
  • Brand-to-Creator Evolution: Deeper collaborations like co-created content.
  • Audience-to-Ecosystem Trust: The brand itself becomes a trusted signal.
